About

Hey, this is Junying Wang (王君瑛). I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at CGIT lab in the Department of Computer Science at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Ulrich Neumann.
Before that, I got my bachelor’s degree from Ocean University of China. When I studied at USC, I was glad to work in Vision and Graphics Lab of USC ICT, advised by Prof. Hao Li.
If you want to know more about me, check my CV here.


Research Topics

My main focus is on digital human research, including computer graphics and computer vision. I’m also interested in game design! Technology may not yet be capable of human consciousness, so with the limited time that I have on Earth, I want to introduce technology that brings innovation, equality, and mutual support to society. Currently, my research focuses on 3D reconstruction, neural rendering, and image synthesis.
