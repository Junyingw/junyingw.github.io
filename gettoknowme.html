About Me

Hey, this is Junying Wang (王君瑛), I am a current master student of University of Southern California, majoring in Computer Science (Multimedia and Creative Technology). I got my bachelor degree from Ocean University of China. When I study at USC, I am glad to work in Vision and Graphics Lab of USC ICT with Prof. Hao Li, and also thanks for my direct supervisors Dr. Weikai Chen and Dr. Mingming He's instructions!

My main focus is on digital human research area, including computer graphics and computer vision. I am also interested in game design! Technology may not yet be capable of human consciousness, so with the limited time that I have on Earth, I want to introduce technology that brings innovation, equality, and mutual support to the whole society. Currently, I am working on motion transfer research topic. In the future, I aim to optimize the digital human part to achieve motion beyond the vision.
